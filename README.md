# 📖 Project Overview

Every e-commerce business faces the same challenge:

> Thousands of users visit the website, but only a small percentage complete a purchase.

Understanding **where customers leave**, **why they leave**, and **what drives conversions** is one of the most important business problems.

This project performs an **end-to-end E-Commerce Funnel Analysis** using Python to identify customer behavior, optimize the sales funnel, and generate actionable business insights.

Rather than simply creating charts, this project focuses on answering real business questions using data.

---

# 🎯 Business Objectives

The analysis aims to answer questions such as:

- Where do customers drop off in the purchase journey?
- Which marketing channels drive the highest conversions?
- Which regions perform best?
- Does device type influence conversion?
- Which product categories generate the most revenue?
- What time of day has the highest conversion rate?
- How can conversion rates be improved?

# 📌 Key Business Insights

✅ Customer conversion decreases at every funnel stage.

✅ The Purchase stage experiences the highest customer drop-off.

✅ High traffic does not always generate high revenue.

✅ Marketing channel performance varies significantly.

✅ Regional customer behavior differs.

✅ Device type influences purchasing decisions.

✅ Product categories contribute differently to revenue.

✅ Customer engagement changes across different days and hours.

---

# 💡 Business Recommendations

- Optimize the checkout process.
- Reduce customer drop-off at critical funnel stages.
- Invest more in high-converting marketing channels.
- Improve mobile user experience.
- Focus on high-performing product categories.
- Launch campaigns during peak conversion hours.
- Implement cart abandonment recovery strategies.
